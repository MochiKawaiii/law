Employment

Our employment practice helps businesses navigate Vietnam's complex labor law landscape. We provide comprehensive legal support for all aspects of employer-employee relationships, ensuring compliance while protecting your business interests.

From hiring foreign workers to managing workforce restructuring, our team provides practical guidance tailored to your specific industry and business needs.

Our Services Include:

  • Work permit and visa applications for foreign workers
  • Employment contract drafting and review
  • Internal labor regulations and policies
  • Collective bargaining agreements
  • Termination and severance advisory
  • Labor dispute resolution
  • Social insurance and benefits compliance
  • Workplace health and safety regulations
