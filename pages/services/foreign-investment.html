Foreign Investment in Vietnam

Henrison Law provides comprehensive legal services to assist foreign investors in establishing and expanding their business presence in Vietnam. Our experienced team guides you through every step of the investment process, from initial market entry strategy to ongoing compliance.

Vietnam has become one of the most attractive investment destinations in Southeast Asia, offering a young workforce, competitive costs, and a strategic location. However, navigating the regulatory landscape requires expert legal guidance to ensure compliance and protect your investment.

Our Services Include:

  • Investment Registration Certificate (IRC) application and amendment
  • Enterprise Registration Certificate (ERC) application
  • Foreign-owned company establishment (100% FDI or joint venture)
  • Representative office and branch establishment
  • Business Cooperation Contracts (BCC)
  • Investment licensing for conditional business sectors
  • Real estate and construction project licensing
  • Post-licensing compliance and reporting

Our track record includes successfully assisting hundreds of foreign companies from USA, Europe, Japan, Korea, Australia, and other countries enter the Vietnamese market. We understand the unique challenges faced by foreign investors and provide tailored solutions to meet your specific needs.
